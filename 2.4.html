Vibrational and rotational spectroscopy of diatomic molecules.

1. Introduction

Vibrational and rotational spectroscopy studies the motion of molecules based on infrared (IR) and microwave absorption:

Rotational Spectroscopy (Microwave Region, 1–100 cm⁻¹) → Studies pure rotational transitions.

Vibrational Spectroscopy (Infrared Region, 400–4000 cm⁻¹) → Examines vibrational transitions, often coupled with rotational motion (ro-vibrational spectra).
